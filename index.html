Our Story

Founded in 2018, Epic Lab began as a small passion project by sneaker enthusiasts who were frustrated with the lack of quality shoe cleaning services in Jakarta. What started as a hobby quickly grew into a professional service as word spread about our exceptional results.

Today, we're proud to be Jakarta's premier shoe cleaning and restoration service, trusted by sneakerheads, professionals, and everyday shoe lovers alike. Our team combines technical expertise with genuine care for footwear to deliver results that consistently exceed expectations.

Our Mission

To extend the life of your favorite footwear through professional care and restoration, reducing waste and helping you get the most value from your shoe investments.
